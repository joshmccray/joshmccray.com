## Overview

Compassion International needed a cohesive icon system that could communicate complex concepts across their digital products while remaining true to their mission of serving children in poverty worldwide. I designed a custom product icon library that balances warmth and humanity with clarity and scalability.

## The Challenge

Existing icon solutions fell short for Compassion's unique needs. Generic icon libraries felt cold and corporate, failing to capture the warmth of an organization focused on children. The icons needed to:

- **Feel human and approachable** rather than sterile and technical
- **Represent global perspectives** avoiding Western-centric visual assumptions
- **Scale across contexts** from small UI elements to large marketing materials
- **Align with brand identity** complementing Compassion's existing visual language

![Human curves in icon design](/case-studies/compassion-icons/icon-1.png)

## Design Principles

### Human Touch

Organic curves add warmth to the icons, alluding to the curve of a smile. This subtle design choice ensures the icons feel approachable and friendly rather than rigid and mechanical.

![Human curves in icon design](/case-studies/compassion-icons/icon-2.png)

### G-local Representation

One of the most important considerations was avoiding Western-centric assumptions. For example, when depicting food, a Western lens might default to a plate with fork and knife. But for Compassion's global audience, a bowl better represents how many cultures around the world eat.

![Global and local representation](/case-studies/compassion-icons/icon-3.png)

### Simplicity at Scale

Icons must remain legible at small sizes while retaining character at larger scales. I avoided the overuse of detail, ensuring each icon reads clearly whether displayed as a 16px interface element or a large marketing graphic.

![Simplicity in icon design](/case-studies/compassion-icons/icon-4.png)

### Brand Typography Connection

The icon system draws inspiration from Compassion's brand typeface, Neighbour Sans. Tapered end caps add an organic feel that mirrors the typography, creating visual harmony across all brand touchpoints.

![Brand typeface connection](/case-studies/compassion-icons/icon-5.png)

## The Icon Library

The final library includes icons representing Compassion's core program areas:

- **Relationships** - Mentorship, community, family connections
- **Spiritual Development** - Faith, church, spiritual growth
- **Physical Health** - Healthcare, nutrition, well-being
- **Education** - Learning, school supplies, literacy
- **Life Skills** - Practical abilities for future success

Each icon was designed with three weight variants to support different contexts and UI needs.

## Integration with Design System

The icon library integrates seamlessly with Compassion's broader design system, using consistent stroke weights, sizing conventions, and color tokens. This ensures icons maintain visual consistency whether used in mobile apps, web interfaces, or print materials.

## Impact

The custom icon library now serves as a foundational element of Compassion's digital product experience, helping communicate complex program concepts in an instantly recognizable, culturally sensitive, and brand-aligned way.
